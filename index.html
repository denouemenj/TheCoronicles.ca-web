ABOUT US

The Coronicles is an initiative organized by local Moncton, New Brunswick students working to collect COVID related artworks and literatures from Canadian youth to be published.

LEARN MORE

VISUAL ART

Original visual art pieces such as painting, drawing, photography, and etc. created during the pandemic are encouraged. A piece of explanation for the artwork will increase your chance to be published.

I am interested

LITERATURE

The content and format will be the choice of your own. Let it be a poetry, essay, journal, or literally anything written that is important to you during this pandemic. Make sure your writing is 1000 words to less.
